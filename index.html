HubSpot & Pipeline Skills
🔍 Look up a client on HubSpotDeal history + pipeline + last contact▼
Paste this prompt into Claude, replacing [client name]:
Look up [client name] on HubSpot and give me a summary of their deal history, pipeline value, and last contact date.
Returns: all deals, total pipeline value, most recent activity date, deal stages, owner. Works for any brand in CRM.
⏰ Check open deals — flag stale onesOpen deals by stage + 14-day flag▼
Use this to audit any seller's pipeline:
Check HubSpot for any open deals assigned to [seller name]. List them by stage and flag anything that hasn't been updated in the last 14 days.
Returns: all open deals grouped by pipeline stage, with red flags on anything stale. Great for Friday standups.
📊 Full CRM audit for a sellerAll missing fields, contacts, companies▼
Run this to get a complete missing-data report:
Find all of [seller name]'s missing data on CRM. Check every open deal for: missing contacts, missing companies, overdue close dates, empty next steps, and no description. Give me a priority action list.
Returns: deal-by-deal breakdown of gaps, red/amber priority list, estimated fix time per seller.

🎯 Brand fit research for a pitch3 reasons + audience + social + campaigns▼

Research [brand name] and give me 3 reasons why they'd be a good fit for Pubity Group's social channels. Include their audience, current social presence, and any recent campaigns.
